~ Childhood-fears-in-my-Basement ~




Description

Experimental short film - Multimedia presentation 210325


This experimental short film is a fleeting glance of the childhood fears in the adult life. Sometimes childhood fears (like being in the dark, alone, the reflection of ourselves in the mirror etc.) still exist buried deep in an adult and are triggered by uncomfortable situations.

This short film can be taken as is, although each one can perceive it in a different way by looking through the prism of his own childhood fears.


For the audio synthesis the SuperCollider framework was used. Subtractive synthesis technique was used extensively as well as modulation (RM, PM, FM) and additive synthesis. The music composition is actually a recording of a short live coding session in which some parts were chosen and adapted according to the cinematic shots.
